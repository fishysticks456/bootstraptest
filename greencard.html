Marriage + Green Card = 1 Stop

If you are US citizen, and would like to petition a green card for your spouse, we can have your marriage and green card processed at the same time. Your spouse will get his or her Green Card interview in 3-4 months.

Contact Us



Required Documents

For US Citizens

  1. Naturalization certificate or US birth certificate or US passport
  2. Driver license
  3. Divorce paper or death certificate or annulment (if any)
  4. Marriage certificate (if you are already married)
  5. 1 passport photo
  6. Income tax return from last year, W-2 file, and employment letter

Beneficiary (Alien)

  1. Passport + I-94 Card
  2. Driver license, social security card (if any)
  3. Divorce paper or death certificate or annulment (if any)
  4. Birth certificate
  5. 3 passport photos
  6. Medical report from INS designated civil surgeon (I will arrange this for you at cost $130-$160)
