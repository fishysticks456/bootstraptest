Mobile Savvy

This page was created using AMP, Google's Accelerated Mobile Pages library. It ensures the page can load quickly on mobile by blocking most JS and only allowing basic functionality on the page: text, pictures, slides, menus, ads, etc. Implementing Google's AMP ensures your website doesn't turn away mobile users on a slow network who can't load the page within 3 seconds, which is the threshold for most webpage abandonments.

The coolest thing about AMP is even though it's a boiled down webpage at its barest bones, it still keeps state binding with amp-bind, so you can still dynamically hide and show content not provided by the standard AMP frameworks. They should rewrite the tutorial for AMP to mention this, it took me a long time to find it.
